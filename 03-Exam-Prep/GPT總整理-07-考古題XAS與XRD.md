# 題目 1：XAS 圖譜判讀

**Left figures show the X-ray absorption and emission energies and X-ray absorption spectroscopy (XAS) results of material with different oxygen environment.**
**(a) Please describe the full name of XAS results including element and absorption edge.**
**(b) Please describe the full name of XAS results including element and absorption edge.**

## 解答

### (a)

圖 (a) 的 photon energy 大約在 **845-875 eV**。
對應到 **Ni 的 L-edge**，兩組峰分別對應：
- **Ni L3 edge**（約 852-855 eV）
- **Ni L2 edge**（約 869-872 eV）

答案：**Nickel L-edge X-ray Absorption Spectroscopy (Ni L2,3-edge XAS)**

---

### (b)

圖 (b) 的 photon energy 大約在 **7700-7785 eV**。
對應到 **Co K-edge**（Co K-edge 約 7709 eV）。

參考值：
- Fe K-edge 約 7112 eV
- Co K-edge 約 7709 eV
- Ni K-edge 約 8333 eV

答案：**Cobalt K-edge X-ray Absorption Spectroscopy (Co K-edge XAS)**

更精確可寫：**Co K-edge XANES**（因為看的是吸收邊附近的 near-edge 結構）

---

## 題 1 整理

- **(a) Ni L2,3-edge XAS** = Nickel L-edge X-ray Absorption Spectroscopy
- **(b) Co K-edge XAS** = Cobalt K-edge X-ray Absorption Spectroscopy

# 題目 2：XRD 是非題

**Which of the following statement(s) regarding X-ray Diffraction (XRD) is (are) incorrect?**

### (a) XRD utilizes the constructive interference produced by X-rays interacting with regularly arranged atoms in a crystal.

**正確。** XRD 就是利用晶體中規則排列原子造成的建設性干涉，符合 Bragg's law：nλ = 2d sinθ

### (b) If a sample is a completely amorphous glass, sharp diffraction peaks will appear in the pattern.

**錯誤。** 完全非晶質（amorphous）玻璃沒有長程有序排列，不會出現尖銳的 diffraction peaks，只會出現 broad hump（寬廣散射峰）。

### (c) To observe diffraction at the atomic level, the wavelength of the X-rays used should be in the same order of magnitude as the interatomic spacing.

**正確。** 原子間距大約是 A 等級（10⁻¹⁰ m），X 光波長也要在這個尺度才能有效產生繞射。

## 答案

**Incorrect statement: (b)**

# 題目 3：Cu Kα 波長

**Cu targets are commonly used as X-ray light sources in laboratories. What is the wavelength of Cu Kα?**

## 答案

**Cu Kα wavelength = 1.54 A**

更精確：**1.5406 A = 0.15406 nm**
